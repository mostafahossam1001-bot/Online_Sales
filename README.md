Online Sales Dashboard Analysis
Market Balance (Regional Performance):

Insight: Revenue is highly diversified across European markets. Portugal leads with 8.51%, followed closely by Belgium (8.47%) and Germany (8.46%). No single country dominates the market.

Decision: Maintain the multi-country marketing strategy. Since the margins are close, focus on improving customer retention in "Portugal" and "Belgium" to solidify their lead.

Sales Seasonality:

Insight: A significant sales peak occurs during the Spring, specifically in March (9.16%) and May (9.14%), while November shows the lowest performance (7.29%).

Decision: Allocate the bulk of the advertising budget and "New Arrivals" campaigns to February and April to capitalize on the Spring rush. Use November for "Clearance Sales" to move slow stock.

Returns & Logistics Optimization:

Insight: The "Furniture" category is the top revenue generator but also has the highest return rate. Additionally, the average shipping cost is $17.48 per order.

Decision: Investigate the quality and packaging of Furniture items to reduce returns. To improve the Average Order Value (currently $12.66), offer "Free Shipping" on bundles or orders above a certain threshold.
